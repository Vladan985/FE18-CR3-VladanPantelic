Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso

Spend an evening at one of the most elegant venues and enjoy a soft drink followed by a three-course meal from a set menu

Il Gambero Rossi specialises in occasion dining, aiming to do things properly with the manners and trappings of tradition warmed by friendliness and the personal touch. Every meal should be a celebration. Open for lunch and dinner seven days a week, the restaurant is located on Food Street, just off bustling Stakehousegate a few yards from Vegetarian Street Station. The meat comes from world-renowned royal butcher "The Metzger" with whom the venue has a long-standing and personal connection. Vegetarian and vegan diners are welcome and they can find both established classics and daily specials to cater to their dietary preferences.
